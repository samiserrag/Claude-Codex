Design notes (mockup-only — not in real cockpit)

Differences from current Polaris (in .agent-handoff/DASHBOARD.html):

  • Single panel, not three concurrent. MVP scope per advisory audit.
  • No outcome-circle progress rings. Replaced with text step indicator ("Step 5 of 5 · Closed satisfied") to avoid filled-ring-implies-approved confusion.
  • Role surfaced first, above the decision itself. Answers "am I needed?" before showing what.
  • Approval boundary is a first-class block, not buried as a footer disclaimer. "This does NOT authorize" enumerated explicitly per the differentiator discipline.
  • No vanity metrics: no Bootstrap Progress %, Lane Health graphs, throughput, lead time, agents-online tiles. Deferred until proven needed.
  • No notifications icon, no multi-lane switcher. One decision, one screen.
  • Tone: system font stack, no gradients, no glow effects, muted accents. Less "AI demo," more "tool I trust at 11pm."
  • Evidence collapsed to 3 bullets + "View full audit →" link. Detail-on-demand instead of always-visible.
  • Persistent footer carries the 3 anti-sycophancy reminders verbatim.

What this mockup intentionally omits:

  • Decision Detail middle panel (deferred — accessed via "View full audit")
  • Operations Board / secondary Kanban (deferred — accessed via separate "View all circles" link not shown here)
  • Outcome Circle progress ring visualization (avoided — text-only step indicator instead)
  • Real-time agent status indicators (no agents-online tiles)
  • Snooze / delegate / drag-drop interactions (must remain no-touch per audit)
